Our story

Why we built this

NexCode started with a familiar frustration. As Lua developers ourselves, we watched scripts we'd poured months into get cracked, leaked, and resold within days of release — sometimes hours. The tools available forced an impossible choice: use an obfuscator with no licensing, or a key system with no real protection.

So you'd bolt together three or four services that were never designed to work together. The obfuscator didn't know about your keys. Your key system couldn't protect your webhooks. And none of it talked to your Discord, where your actual community lived.

We thought there had to be a better way — one platform where protection and licensing were designed together from day one. A custom VM that changes with every build. HWID-locked keys that actually stop sharing. Encrypted webhooks. A REST API for automation. And a Discord bot so you can run the whole thing where your users already are.

That's NexCode. Today it protects thousands of scripts for developers across Roblox, FiveM, and the wider Lua ecosystem — from solo creators shipping their first paid script to studios running serious businesses.

What we believe

Our principles

🎯

Honesty over hype

No security is unbreakable, and we'll never claim otherwise. We tell you exactly what our protection does and doesn't do, so you can make informed decisions.

⚡

Developer experience first

Powerful tools that are painful to use don't get used. We obsess over making protection something you set up in minutes, not weeks.

🔒

Your work is yours

We're custodians of your intellectual property, not owners of it. Your source is never stored in plaintext, and your data stays yours.
